About us

      Taoyuan Children's Art Museum serves children as the main purpose. The orientations are "Children" "Art" and "Digital." It uses "Art" and "Books" to present as a dual spindle. Hoping children's art museum can become the cradle of art in Taoyuan City in order to create the growth cycle of "Inspiration, Learning, Creation, Development, and Contribution". And then enhance the cultural conservation and competitiveness of children in Taoyuan City.
